Research Onboarding

Team Members: Zhitao Yu, Yuxuan Mei, Rahul Chandra, Dhruv Yadav

We provide a tool for users interested in finding collaborators in fields/topics they are interested in within Computer Science. The tool visualizes an author network graph based on the publications data from DBLP since 2010 (inclusive). Dropdown menus for venues and authors are provided, along with filters on publication year, citation count, number of papers published, and number of collaborations. If a specific author interests you, you can click on their name to go to their author page to see more relevant information such as their contact information, most cited papers, and trends of their research topics. Happy exploring!
